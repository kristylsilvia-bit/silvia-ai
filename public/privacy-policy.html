Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Silvia AI is a chat studio that helps you interact with supported AI models. This policy explains what information may be handled when you use the app.

Information You Provide

You may enter chat messages, upload files, or sign in with an authentication provider. The content you provide is used to deliver the chat experience and related app features.

Local Storage

The app may store chat history, theme preferences, model preferences, and free-trial usage information in your browser's local storage so the experience persists between visits.

Authentication

If you sign in, the app may receive basic account information from the authentication provider, such as your display name, email address, profile image, and user identifier.

AI Processing

Messages and attachments you submit may be sent to third-party AI services configured for Silvia AI so those services can generate responses. Do not submit sensitive information unless you are comfortable with that processing.

Third-Party Services

Silvia AI may rely on services such as Firebase, Google AI/Gemini APIs, hosting providers, and font providers. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Controls

You can clear chat history from inside the app. You can also clear this site's browser storage through your browser settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Silvia AI project owner through the repository at github.com/kristylsilvia-bit/silvia-ai.
